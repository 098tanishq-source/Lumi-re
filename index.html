Our Story

Born from
a Passion for
Real Flavour

Founded in 1998, Lumière began as a quiet bistro on a cobblestone street. Today it stands as one of the city's most celebrated tables — yet the soul remains unchanged. Every dish carries the memory of where it started.

  • Locally sourced, seasonal ingredients from partner farms
  • Family recipes reimagined for the modern palate
  • Zero-waste kitchen commitment since 2018
  • Warm, unhurried hospitality — always

Project Overview

Behind the Design

A breakdown of the design thinking, strategy, and technical execution behind Lumière's digital presence.

01
Goal
Build a premium restaurant website that converts visitors into reservations. Communicate elegance, quality, and trust through design alone — before the customer sets foot inside.
ConversionTrustBrand
02
Target Audience
Urban professionals, couples, and corporate clients aged 28–55. Mobile-first — most discovery happens via Instagram. Decision made on visual credibility and social proof.
UrbanMobile-firstPremium
03
Design Direction
Dark romantic luxury. Playfair Display for editorial intimacy paired with Manrope for precision. Gold accent — restrained and intentional. Candlelight and velvet.
Dark RomanticSerif + Sans
04
Responsive Strategy
Three breakpoints: 1024px, 768px, 480px. Fluid container system. No fixed widths. No horizontal scroll on any device. Section padding scales gracefully.
3 BreakpointsFluid
05
Tools Used
Pure HTML5 + CSS3 + Vanilla JS. Zero frameworks. Google Fonts. Unsplash photography. WhatsApp Business API for reservations. Single-file GitHub Pages deployment.
HTML5CSS3Vanilla JSWhatsApp
06
Key Interactions
Sticky glass nav. Subtle scroll reveals. WhatsApp reservation form with validation. Drink order links. Animated counters. Menu tabs. No loader — no overanimation. Motion nearly invisible.
Scroll RevealValidationWhatsApp
